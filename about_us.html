Meet the Team!

We are all volunteers! and are involved in the Oval Ski Club because of our interest in the sport.

The club has an elected committee and several qualified instructors. However we also require additional helpers to make the club a success. If you are interested in helping out with the club on either a Saturday morning or Sunday evening then please contact one of the committee members or instructors (see below).
